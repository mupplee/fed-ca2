Our Origins

Singapore Polytechnic was established on October 27, 1954, as Singapore's first polytechnic.

What started as a small five-story structure on Prince Edward Road has grown into Singapore's largest polytechnic. Singapore Polytechnic established in the heart of the business sector. Today, the polytechnic is conveniently placed on Dover Road, in the educational belt and science cluster in Singapore's western region. Our graduates have made important contributions to Singapore's economic and industrial progress, with many becoming well-known entrepreneurs. Others hold positions of responsibility and leadership in industry, business, and government organisations.

Singapore's largest polytechnic is a vast campus of 38 hectares of lush nature with over 230,000 renowned alumni. Our 70th anniversary in 2024 is a time for reflection and celebration, but it also marks the beginning of a new age, with more to learn and do. This is SP at 70.

SP70 Moments

On 31 October 2023, SP launched its 70th anniversary book titled 'For All Ages, Singapore Polytechnic At Seventy' at the Polytechnic-ITE (POLITE) Staff Appreciation Day. The book chronicles the evolution of polytechnic education in Singapore and encapsulates SP's rich history as the nation's first polytechnic, its achievements, aspirations, and dedication to lifelong learning. The occasion, which was graced by Guest-of-Honour Minister for Education Mr Chan Chun Sing, also marked the commencement of SP's 70th anniversary celebrations.

The SP campus was abuzz this year at our 70th Anniversary Open House. Visitors were treated to school tours to visit our cutting-edge facilities and were thrilled by exciting CCA performances at the Plaza. We also had a very special drone swarming display as part of our inaugural SP Industry & Innovation Showcase.

As part of SP's 70th Anniversary, we held a music festival at SP. Despite the rain and drizzle, the spirit of those who attended was not dampened. Thousands danced to the music, especially when local artists like Evan Low, Rangga Jones, and Marian Carmel took to the stage. The festival’s aim was to cast a glow on the importance of youth mental wellness and to reinforce that music and mental well-being can go hand-in-hand.

SP's 70th anniversary festivities in 2024 include seven major events scheduled throughout the year. The fourth event was a Charity Golf Tournament at Orchid Country Club, followed by a Gala Dinner that highlighted SP's commitment to supporting two initiatives: financially challenged students in SP and assisting other communities in need. On July 12, 2024, around 1,000 workers, students, industry partners, alumni, donors, friends, and some President's Challenge grantees attended both celebrations.

Read our book For All Ages

In honour of SP's 70th anniversary, we have released a commemorative book titled 'For All Ages, Singapore Polytechnic At Seventy', which features some of the individuals and events that have shaped us over the years.
